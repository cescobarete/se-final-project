RULES

YAHTZEE consists of a number of rounds(13 ROUNDS)
A player can get up to THREE rolls with 5 die.
OPTIONAL: You can chose to end your turn before your 3rd roll.
The total score is calculated by summing all categories

UPPER section(categories)
Aces: The sum of the dice with the number 1
Twos: The sum of the dice with the number two
Threes: The sum of the dice with the number three
Fours: The sum of the dice with the number four
Fives: The sum of the dice with the number five
Sixes: The sum if the dice with the number six
A bonus of 35 is added if 63 or more points are scored in the upper section

LOWER section
Three of a kind: At least three dice the same, sum of dice(points)
Four of a kind: At least four fice the same, sum of dice(points)
Full House: Three of one number and two of another, 25 points
Small straight: Four sequential dice ex: 1,2,3,4, 30 points
Large straight: Five sequential dice ex: 1,2,3,4,5, 40 points
YAHTZEE: All five dice the same ex: 1,1,1,1,1, 50 points
Chance: Any combination, Sum of all dice
